Terme-à-LLOD.

Terminology Databases as a Service. Terme-à-LLOD supports the conversion and deployment of TermBase eXchange (TBX) files as linked data. The project utilizes recent advances in containerization in order to easily expose the efforts of a curated terminology database towards a broader audience. It integrates with projects such as the European Language Grid (ELG) and Prêt-à-LLOD.

Instances

These TBX terminology databases have been converted as part of our initial case studies:

IATE

IATE (Interactive Terminology for Europe) is the EU's terminology database.
Courtesy of the Translation Centre for the Bodies of the European Union, the IATE Terminology combines several legacy databases as well as multi-lingual terminology for all aspects of the European Union.

GenTerm

Gent university's Terminology Centre (CvT) offers several terminologies in TBX format that cover a variety of topics:

  • intaglio printmaking; English and Dutch (Jasmien Roelens, 2007)
  • solar panels; English and Dutch (Gregory Depuydt, 2010)
  • medical (diseases, symptoms); English and Dutch (Mercedes D'Hoker, 2014)
  • pharmaceutical (names of substances; and key terminology as used by the European Medicines Agency); English and Dutch (via BCFI and OPUS)
  • waste management Life Cycle Assessment (LCA); English and Dutch (Rianne van Eldik, Universiteit Utrecht, 2019)
